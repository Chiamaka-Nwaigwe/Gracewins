Biography

About Me

I am Chiamaka Ngozika Nwaigwe, a Project Management Professional based in Hamilton, Ontario. Grounded in informatics and healthcare technology, I lead full project lifecycles across healthcare IT and education sectors, from requirements gathering through implementation and validation.

I am known for turning complex organisational requirements into structured, deliverable outcomes. I bring analytical rigour and evidence-based thinking to every engagement, drawing on a background that spans healthcare program coordination, behavioural support, and broadcast media to offer a cross-functional perspective that few project managers bring to the table.

I hold a Master of Professional Studies in Informatics with a specialisation in Health Informatics and Project Management from Northeastern University, and I continue to grow through professional certifications and active industry engagement.

Lead enterprise-level programme management and digital health transformation, championing evidence-based, people-centred processes that redefine how healthcare organisations deliver care at scale.

02 — Research & Digital Strategy · Contributor★ Major Highlight

Kukinpot — Digital Transformation Strategy

Kukinpot is a growing restaurant that had built a loyal customer base but was held back by outdated communication methods and limited digital presence. Without online ordering, a structured management system, or a visible web presence, the business was losing ground to digitally enabled competitors.

Digital TransformationGap AnalysisTechnology EvaluationCompetitive BenchmarkingChange ManagementRoadmap Design

Prioritised digital transformation roadmap delivered, covering POS, inventory, CRM, and online ordering, giving Kukinpot a clear, realistic path to modernise operations

Problem

Kukinpot lacked online ordering, a structured management system, and a meaningful web presence — losing competitive ground to rivals like Papaye and Chop Shop who already operated online ordering platforms, delivery integrations, and active social media channels.

Approach

Conducted a structured digital assessment to identify key operational gaps affecting customer engagement and order coordination. Benchmarked Kukinpot's current state against established competitors and developed targeted technology recommendations grounded in business need, not just technical possibility.

Outcome

Delivered a clear, prioritised roadmap covering point-of-sale systems, inventory tracking, customer relationship management, and an online ordering platform — each recommendation tied to measurable operational improvement.

Reflection

This project reinforced that the strongest transformation strategies are built on understanding the business first. Technology is only as useful as the clarity of thinking that sits behind it.
